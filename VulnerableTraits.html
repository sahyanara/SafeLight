What makes someone vulnerable?

The most common traits seen in victims are:

1. Low socio-economic status - Specifically those in need of help financially

2. Runaways/Parental Neglect - Those without a support system

3. Homeless Youth

4. Recent Relocation

5. LGBTQ+

6. Mental health/Insecurities
