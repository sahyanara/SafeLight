Our Mission

We are youth working to educate, protect, and equip fellow youth through SafeLight. By designing the interactive, educational website SafeLight, teenagers and their parents/guardians are able to learn key information regarding sex trafficking that is often misunderstood. Instead of simply creating an informational page designed to only inform, the quizzes, videos, and story lines offered throughout the interactive course make the experience more enjoyable and retainable for the public, while developing crucial skills in decision making. Our goal for this educational course is to disprove the misconceptions surrounding sex trafficking and empower youth to protect themselves and make well informed decisions, while simultaneously decreasing their own vulnerability.
