How can I increase my confident?

Everyone has different backgrounds, families, and communities that can affect your confidence levels. The key is to not allow these aspects of your life define you or your self worth. This is easier said than done, but here are a few videos to help you get started. They include tips on how to build confidence in your life in general and a few ted talks. One in particular discusses a story of how despite very unfortunate circumstances, you can keep your confidence and avoid ending up in a bad place, mindset, or even worse circumstance.
